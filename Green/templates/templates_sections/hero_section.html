    With us identification of diseases is more easy

    Early identification of diseases is crucial to avoid huge losses and reduce the excessive use of pesticides, which can harm human health and the environment. In most cases, and especially in developing countries and small farms, farmers identify crop diseases with the naked eye based on visual symptoms. This is a tedious task that requires expertise in plant pathology and excessive treatment time. Thus, this method of visual observation is not practical and feasible for large farms and may even yield erroneous predictions due to biased decisions

    Our main objectives

    Our website aims to help farmers and plant enthusiasts to identify diseases in their plants and provide tips to prevent and manage them.The platform then provides a detailed description of the identified disease and offers useful tips on how to prevent and manage it. Our goal is to empower plant owners to take control of their plant health and reduce the negative impact of diseases on their plants and crops.
