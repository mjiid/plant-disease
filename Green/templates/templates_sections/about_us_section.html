We are data engineers students at INPT. We are passionate about helping people all over the world. This is why we have developed this platform which will detect plant diseases and propose solutions.


What is the INPT school ?


In parallel with the development of solid scientific and technical skills leading to the art of solving complex problems, the INPT aims to instill in future engineers the spirit of leadership. To do this, we encourage them to develop their knowledge of the business world as well as their project management and teamwork skills, all combined with good communication skills and a dynamic student life, friendly and rich in terms of events within our campus.
In order to promote the integration of its graduates into society as a whole, the INPT has adopted a new pedagogical approach which consists in meeting the challenges and meeting the needs of companies in terms of interactivity.
